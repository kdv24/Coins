Users will enter an amount of cents and be shown the smallest number of quarters, dimes, nickels, and pennies needed to make that change.

Test 1-
Input: 1 cent
Output: 1 pennies

Test 2-
Input: 5 cents
Output: 1 nickel

Test 3-
Input: 10 cents
Output: 1 dime

Test 4-
Input: 25 cents
Output: 1 quarter

Test 5-
Input: 6 cents
Output: 1 nickel, 1 pennies

Test 6-
Input: 16 cents
Output: 1 dime, 1 nickel, 1 pennies

Test 7-
Input 46 cents
Output: 1 quarter, 1 dime, 1 nickel, 1 pennies
